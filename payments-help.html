Payments Help

Payment Failed

If your payment failed, retry after checking internet connection and bank balance.

Money Deducted

If money is deducted but order not confirmed, refund usually gets processed automatically within 3-7 days.

UPI Problem

Make sure your UPI app is updated and bank servers are working properly before retrying.

COD Not Available

Cash on Delivery may not be available for certain locations or high-value orders.
