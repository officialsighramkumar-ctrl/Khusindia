Refund & Cancellation

Understand cancellation, return and refund rules for orders placed on KHUSINDIA.

Cancellation Policy

Customers may request order cancellation within 7 days of placing the order.

Cancellation requests may not be accepted if the order has already been processed, packed, shipped or marked as out for delivery by the seller or logistics partner.

In such situations, customers may refuse to accept the order at the time of delivery wherever applicable.

Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged, defective or incorrect product, you must contact KHUSINDIA customer support within 7 days of delivery.

Requests will be reviewed after verification by the seller, merchant or logistics partner. Supporting images or videos may be required for verification.

Product Not as Expected

If the delivered product differs significantly from the description or images displayed on the Platform, customers should notify customer support within 7 days of receiving the order.

After reviewing the complaint, KHUSINDIA may approve a replacement, return or refund depending on the nature of the issue.

Non-Returnable Products

Certain products may not be eligible for return or cancellation due to hygiene, personal care, consumable, customized or other applicable reasons. Such conditions will be mentioned on the respective product page wherever applicable.

Manufacturer Warranty

Products covered under manufacturer warranty should be serviced or claimed directly through the manufacturer’s authorized service centers as per their warranty terms.

Refund Process

Once a refund request is approved, the refund amount will be processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

In case of Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, customers may be required to provide valid bank account details for refund processing.

Refund Delays

Refund timelines may vary depending on banks, payment gateways or financial institutions. KHUSINDIA shall not be responsible for delays caused by third-party payment systems.

Fraud Prevention

KHUSINDIA reserves the right to refuse returns, cancellations or refunds in cases involving suspicious activity, abuse of policies or fraudulent transactions.

Contact Support

For cancellation, return or refund related assistance, users may contact KHUSINDIA support through the Help Center available on the Platform.
