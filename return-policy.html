Return & Exchange Policy

Read our return and exchange guidelines for products purchased through KHUSINDIA.

Return Eligibility

Customers may request a return or exchange within 5-7 days from the date of delivery.

Requests made after the return window may not be accepted unless required under applicable law.

Conditions for Returns

To qualify for a return or exchange, the product must be unused, unwashed and in the same condition as received.

The item must be returned with its original packaging, tags, accessories, invoice and all accompanying materials.

Damaged or Defective Products

Products received in damaged, defective or incorrect condition may be eligible for replacement or return after inspection and approval by KHUSINDIA or the respective seller.

Customers may be required to provide clear images or videos of the issue for verification.

Non-Returnable Products

Certain products including personal care items, consumables, customized products, innerwear or hygiene-sensitive products may not be eligible for return or exchange.

Products purchased during special sales, clearance events or promotional offers may also be excluded from return eligibility, wherever specifically mentioned.

Exchange Process

Approved replacement or exchanged products are generally delivered within 7–10 business days, depending on product availability and delivery location.

Inspection & Approval

Once the returned item is received, it will undergo quality inspection and verification.

Customers will be notified through email, SMS or other available communication methods regarding approval or rejection of the request.

Refund for Returned Products

If a return request is approved and replacement is unavailable, the refund amount will be processed to the original payment method within a reasonable timeframe.

Policy Abuse

KHUSINDIA reserves the right to refuse returns, exchanges or refunds in cases involving suspicious activity, excessive return behavior or misuse of the return policy.

Need Help?

For return or exchange related assistance, users may contact KHUSINDIA customer support through the Help Center available on the Platform.
