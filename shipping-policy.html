Shipping Policy

Learn about order processing, shipping timelines and delivery information for KHUSINDIA orders.

Order Processing

Orders placed on KHUSINDIA are processed after successful payment confirmation and order verification.

Orders may take additional processing time during sales, festivals, public holidays or high order volume periods.

Shipping Partners

Orders are shipped through registered domestic courier companies, logistics partners or Speed Post services.

Delivery Timeline

Deliveries are generally completed within 7–10 business days from the date of order confirmation or payment, depending on the delivery location and product availability.

Estimated delivery timelines displayed during checkout are indicative and may vary due to operational or logistical reasons.

Delivery Address

All orders will be delivered to the shipping address provided by the customer at the time of placing the order.

Customers are responsible for providing accurate and complete shipping details. KHUSINDIA shall not be liable for failed deliveries caused by incorrect address information.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, will be displayed during checkout before order confirmation.

Any shipping or handling charges paid during purchase are generally non-refundable unless otherwise stated.

Delivery Delays

KHUSINDIA is not responsible for delays caused by courier companies, transportation issues, weather conditions, public holidays, natural disasters or other events beyond reasonable control.

Order Tracking

Customers may receive shipment confirmation and tracking details through email, SMS or other available communication methods once the order is dispatched.

Failed Deliveries

If delivery attempts fail due to customer unavailability, incorrect address or refusal to accept the package, the order may be returned or cancelled subject to applicable policies.

Contact Support

For shipping or delivery related assistance, users may contact KHUSINDIA customer support through the Help Center available on the Platform.
